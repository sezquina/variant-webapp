ABraOM

Arquivo Brasileiro Online de Mutações

Brazilian Online Archive of Mutations

This databank represents a survey of the genetic diversity of Brazil. A total of 2,382,573 variants were called from 600 exomes across a healthy cohort from Sao Paulo. 2,366,230 (99.3%) of our variants were found in ClinVAR. 743,061 (31.2%) of our variants were found in ANNOVAR. Please refer to the about page for more detailed summary statistics.

We offer access to this data via the web form above and in the future will provide programmatical support via a REST API. Access will be rate limited. Please see the terms of use for more information.
